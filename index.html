bass player of the month

George Gardner

Hailing from the East of England, George Gardner has always had a passion for the low-end of music. He studied jazz bass at Leeds College of Music and had been a part of many musical outfits. George is most notably the bass player of London-based post-rock band Famous, he is known for his unorthodox methods of playing, his raucos on-stage presence and of course his Ernie Ball Music Man 5 string bass.
